Common Questions

Pricing FAQ

Is there really no credit card required?

Correct. Start your 30-day free trial with just your email. No card until you decide to continue.

What happens if I exceed my monthly transport limit?

We'll notify you when you're close. You can upgrade instantly or we'll prorate additional transport volume. We never cut you off mid-month.

Can I change plans anytime?

Yes. Upgrade or downgrade at any time. Changes take effect on your next billing cycle. No penalties, no locked-in contracts.

Do you offer annual billing discounts?

Yes — annual billing saves 2 months (roughly 17% off). Contact us after signup to switch to annual billing.

What payment methods do you accept?

All major credit/debit cards via Stripe. ACH bank transfer available on Elite plans.

Is there a setup fee?

No setup fees on any plan. Professional and Elite include guided onboarding at no extra cost.
