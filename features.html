Smart Intake

Forward an email.
Get a transport record.

Your funeral home partners forward their removal call to your MortuPro email. That's it. Our AI parses every detail — name, pickup, destination, weight, special instructions — into a complete transport record in seconds.

No portal logins for clients. No re-typing data. No missed information. Just a clean transport ready to dispatch.

✓ Accepts forwarded email or fax
✓ AI extracts all transport fields
✓ Auto-matches to known funeral homes
✓ Flags missing or unclear information
✓ Ready to dispatch in < 30 seconds
