Platform Features

Everything your operation needs.
Nothing it doesn't.

Six core systems working together so nothing falls through the cracks — from first call to final invoice.

🚨

Smart Dispatch

New calls hit every driver instantly. Unaccepted calls escalate automatically with SMS re-alerts and voice calls at 1 and 2 minutes. No call ever goes unanswered.

📋

Smart Intake

Drop in a forwarded email or fax. AI parses it into a complete transport record in seconds. No more retyping. No more missed details.

Auto-Billing

Every completed transport auto-calculates cost from your rate card. Generate, send, and collect invoices without touching a calculator. Square payments built in.

Driver App

Third Party Forwarding

Operations Dashboard

Real-time dispatch board, calendar view, fleet status, and revenue reporting in one screen. Know your business at a glance, anytime.

How It Works

From call to invoice
in three steps.

MortuPro handles the whole transport lifecycle so your team can focus on what matters — serving families.

1

Call Comes In

Via portal, forwarded email, or fax. MortuPro auto-parses it into a complete transport record with all necessary details.

2

Drivers Alerted

Every available driver is notified instantly. They accept, navigate, and update status — all from mobile. Escalation is automatic.

3

Transport Complete

Job closes. Invoice auto-generates from your rate card. Sent to the funeral home. You get paid. Repeat.
